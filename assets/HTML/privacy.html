Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Digital Estate Manager ("we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Authentication Information

Asset Information

You may voluntarily store information related to your digital assets, including:

  • Asset Name
  • Platform Name
  • Username or Email
  • Password Hints
  • Notes
  • Assigned Nominees

Nominee Information

You may provide:

  • Nominee Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Relationship Information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the service
  • Manage your digital asset records
  • Manage nominee information
  • Improve user experience
  • Send account-related notifications
  • Enhance security and reliability

3. Password Security

Digital Estate Manager does not require users to store actual passwords.

Users are encouraged to store only password hints or recovery instructions.

4. Data Storage

Your information is stored securely using cloud-based services and industry-standard security practices.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may disclose information only:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and users
  • With your consent

6. User Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of your account
  • Providing accurate information
  • Managing the nominee information you provide

7. Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services such as:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Analytics and infrastructure providers

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide the service.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the application.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support channels provided within the application.
